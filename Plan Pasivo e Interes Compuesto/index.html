What is the composed interest?



Compound interest is an accounting and finance strategy, where it consists of accumulating the revenues or interests of a capital so that others can produce. Compound interest allows compounding of interest periodically, day by day, month by month, and so on.


In short, compound interest allows immediate reinvestment of interest, that is, the use of compound interest allows the initial capital to increase with the same interest it generates.
